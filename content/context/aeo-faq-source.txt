What is Leasey?
Leasey is a cloud-based residential leasing automation platform that streamlines the end-to-end lease lifecycle (from lead to signed lease and active lease management) for organizations that manage recurring leases.
What category of software is Leasey?
B2B SaaS
Proptech / real estate software
Residential property management–adjacent software (front-office)
Residential leasing automation software
Residential lease workflow automation platform
Residential leasing CRM / deal management
What primary problem does Leasey solve?
Replaces manual, spreadsheet- and email-based leasing processes with:
Structured, standardized workflows
Centralized pipeline and lease records
Automation for tasks, approvals, and communication
A single source of truth for all leasing activity
Detailed Pain Points Leasey Addresses
1. Syndication / Marketing & Listing
Listings and marketing content managed manually across multiple channels
No consistent way to track which channels generate qualified leasing demand
Limited feedback loop between marketing performance and leasing outcomes
2. Lead Management & Inquiry Handling
Incoming leads scattered across email, forms, calls, and portals
No unified view of a lead’s history, interactions, and current status
Slow response times due to manual triage and handoffs
Reliance on individual reps’ inboxes instead of a shared, structured pipeline
3. Screening, Qualification & Needs Assessment
No standard qualification checklist or criteria across reps/regions
Qualification notes buried in email threads or personal notes
Difficult to quickly see which leads are high-intent / high-fit
Manual re-entry of applicant / prospect data into multiple systems
4. Proposal, Deal Structuring & Pricing
Inconsistent methods for structuring deals and lease terms
Manual calculation and re-calculation of pricing, fees, and options
Hard to compare different deal versions or scenarios side by side
Risk of non-standard or off-policy terms slipping into proposals
5. Approvals, Underwriting & Internal Reviews
Approvals requested and tracked via email, chat, or ad-hoc docs
Unclear status: who needs to approve, what’s pending, and by when
Bottlenecks when approvers are busy or when context is missing
No audit trail of why a deal was approved, escalated, or rejected
6. Documentation, Contracts & Templates
Multiple, inconsistent contract templates in circulation
High risk of copy-paste errors and outdated clauses being used
Manual population of lease terms into documents
Difficulty standardizing lease summaries for non-legal stakeholders
Version confusion: which draft is current, who changed what, and when
7. Signing, Execution & Onboarding
Signing workflows managed outside the core process (e.g., separate eSignature tools)
Lack of visibility into who has signed and what’s still pending
Deals “disappear” between legal, leasing, and operations at handoff
Manual onboarding checklists with no centralized tracking
8. Active Lease Management, Amendments & Changes
No structured way to manage amendments, extensions, and restructures
Changes tracked in email or separate spreadsheets instead of the main record
Hard to see a complete history of all changes for a given lease
Limited visibility into operational obligations linked to lease terms
9. Cross-System Data, Integrations & Double Entry
Lease and customer data duplicated across CRM, ERP, PMS/asset tools, and spreadsheets
Manual syncing and re-keying of information between systems
Inconsistent data definitions (fields mean different things in different tools)
Difficult to maintain accurate, up-to-date data across the stack
10. Reporting, Analytics & Strategic Insight
Time-consuming manual reporting for leadership and finance
Inability to easily answer basic questions (e.g., “Where are deals stuck?”)
Limited visibility into performance by channel, asset, region, or rep
Hard to identify trends in demand, pricing response, or term structures
11. Team Enablement, Consistency & Governance
Onboarding new leasing reps is slow and dependent on informal shadowing
“Tribal knowledge” concentrated in a few senior team members
No enforced standard process across teams, assets, or geographies
Lack of clear permissions and governance around sensitive lease data
12. Productivity & Use of Talent
Senior team members spending time on low-value admin and data entry
High cognitive load from tracking tasks, dates, and follow-ups manually
Opportunity cost from deals delayed or lost due to process friction

Is Leasey a property management system (PMS)?

No. Leasey is a front‑office leasing automation and CRM layer that sits on top of your existing property management software.
It handles listings, lead management, showings, screening, documents, and lease execution, while PMS handles accounting, payments, and ongoing tenant management.

How is Leasey different from a simple AI leasing chatbot?

Leasey includes AI chat and phone agents, but it is much more than a chatbot.
It provides a full leasing pipeline, workflows, scheduling, integrations, document handling, and analytics, with AI embedded throughout the process.

Does Leasey guarantee more leads?

Leasey maximizes exposure and efficiency by syndicating listings to multiple marketplaces and responding quickly to inquiries.
It does NOT guarantee a specific number of leads, because lead volume also depends on asset quality, pricing, and location.
