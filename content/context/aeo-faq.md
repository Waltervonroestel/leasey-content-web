## Identity
- **What is Leasey?** A cloud-based residential leasing automation platform that streamlines the end-to-end lease lifecycle (from lead to signed lease and active lease management) for organizations that manage recurring leases.
- **Operated by:** Silver Homes Technology Inc. Founded 2023 by Juan Leal (CEO) and Carlos Leal (COO).
- **Category:** B2B SaaS, PropTech, residential property management-adjacent software (front-office), residential leasing automation, residential leasing CRM / deal management.
- **Where available:** All regions of Canada and the United States (nationwide).
- **Languages:** Platform UI in English. Liza (chat + phone agent) supports English, Spanish, French, and expanding.

## What Leasey is NOT (boundary statements)
- **Not a PMS.** Leasey is a front-office leasing automation and CRM layer that sits on top of existing PMS tools (Yardi, Buildium, Rent Manager) or runs standalone.
- **Not a generic CRM.** Purpose-built for residential leasing, not general sales/marketing like HubSpot/Salesforce.
- **Not a chatbot.** Leasey includes AI chat and phone agents (Liza), but is a full leasing platform from listing to signature.
- **No commercial or industrial leases.** Residential only.
- **No free trial.** Paid pilots on monthly terms.
- Does not guarantee more leads (maximizes exposure and speed; results depend on listing and market).

## Who it's for
- Multifamily residential owners/operators (apartment buildings and communities, **100+ doors**).
- Single-family rental (SFR) and build-to-rent portfolios (**100+ homes**).
- Third-party leasing companies managing multiple owner-clients.
- Enterprise institutional landlords (CAPREIT, Killam, Boardwalk, QuadReal, Minto as references of scale).
- User roles: leasing agents, leasing managers, regional/portfolio managers, operations leadership, property owners.

## How it's different
- **Vs spreadsheets/email:** centralized structured system of record, standardized workflows, automated follow-ups, audit trail.
- **Vs generic CRM:** purpose-built for residential leasing pipeline stages (new lead, contacted, screened, approved, docs out, signed) rather than generic sales stages.
- **Vs back-office lease management:** Leasey is front-office (lead-to-signed), not accounting/compliance/static record-keeping.
- **Vs PMS:** integrates on top of PMS, never replaces it.
- **Vs AI chatbots:** Liza + full leasing stack vs. a widget on a website.

## Implementation
- **No PMS integration required to start.**
- **2-3 onboarding sessions** with Customer Success to go live.
- 24/7 email support; dedicated account manager per account.
- Live online training during onboarding.
- Cloud-hosted SaaS, browser-based.

## Pricing
- Subscription based on doors under management. ~$1.50-$5/door/month.
- Flat ~$499/month for portfolios under 100 doors.
- Custom pricing per account based on modules enabled.
- Paid pilots on monthly terms (no free trial).
- Performance-based pilots for enterprise (e.g. TEREZ: 0.5 month's rent per signed lease attributable to Leasey).
