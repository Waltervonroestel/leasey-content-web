# Property management publications (PR + guest post targets)

Mapped outlets for distributing Leasey.AI press releases and guest contributions. Researched 2026-06-08. Refresh periodically with the search button (news-researcher / web).

Use: when a PR or announcement is ready, match it to the right outlet and angle. Always insight-led with attribution, English, no em-dashes.

## Tier 1 — submit press releases / news
- MultifamilyBiz.com — https://www.multifamilybiz.com/pressreleases — Member-posted press releases (executive appointments, financing, acquisitions, product launches, integrations). Best fit for Leasey partnership and product announcements. Has a dedicated PR submission service.
- Multifamily Press — https://www.multifamilypress.com/ — Publishes press releases and news from multifamily and PM industry. Straight news distribution.
- Multi-Housing News — https://www.multihousingnews.com/ — Leader in industry deals and market trends. Guest blog section at /blog/guest-blog/. Pitch data-driven thought leadership, not product pitches.
- Multifamily Dive — https://www.multifamilydive.com/ — Newsletter and site for multifamily executives, ~106,500 decision makers. Editorial, harder to place; pitch a strong data story or trend angle (eg. AI leasing + Fair Housing).

## Tier 2 — community / guest posts
- Multifamily Insiders — https://www.multifamilyinsiders.com/ — Community blog for PMs to exchange ideas. Good for practical, non-salesy thought leadership from Carlos or the company.
- Updater PM blog list and Lodgify list — reference lists of PM blogs to expand outreach. Use to find more guest-post targets.

## What to write per outlet
- PR outlets (MultifamilyBiz, Multifamily Press): formal press release via the press-release agent. Lead with the news + a market datapoint with source. Founder quote. Boilerplate.
- Thought-leadership outlets (Multi-Housing News guest blog, Multifamily Dive, Multifamily Insiders): an insight-led article (not a PR). Lead with a data trend (Yardi vacancy, Frontdesk adoption), develop the operator problem, connect to a positioning pillar. Soft or no product pitch. Author = Carlos (strategy) or Juan (product/tech).
