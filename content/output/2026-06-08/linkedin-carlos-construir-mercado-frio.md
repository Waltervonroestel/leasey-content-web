When we started Leasey, the Canadian market was hot. Units rented almost on their own.

It is easy to build leasing tools when there is a line at the door. The real test comes now, with vacancy climbing nine quarters in a row and rents on new leases turning negative, per Yardi's latest Canadian data.

My brother Juan and I grew up in Colombia, where nobody assumes the customer will just show up. That mindset turned out to be the right one for this moment.

A cold market does not forgive slowness. It forgives even less the team that leaves a lead unanswered.

What I like most about these months is that they force us to prove what we built actually works when filling units is hard. Not when demand is overflowing.

To the operators feeling the shift: you are not alone, and the speed lever is still yours.

Is your stack built for the easy market, or for this one?
