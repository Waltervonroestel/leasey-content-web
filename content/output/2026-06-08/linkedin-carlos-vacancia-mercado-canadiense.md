Vacancy in Canada just hit 5.1% this quarter, according to Yardi's Q1 2026 Multifamily Report. In Calgary it is already 7.3%.

I talk to operators almost every day, and the tone has shifted. A year ago the question was how to push rents higher. Now it is how to stop losing weeks on an empty unit.

In a cooling market, almost nothing is in your control. Demand, rates, migration, you do not move any of that. But the speed at which you respond to a lead, that is yours.

We saw it up close on a 154 unit lease-up in Cochrane, right next to that 7.3% in Calgary. When every inquiry gets answered instantly, instead of waiting until the next morning, vacancy days shrink on their own.

It is not magic. It is just no longer losing the lead you already had.

If your team is answering 30 to 40% of the inquiries coming in, that is your leak, and that is your opportunity.

What are you doing differently this year to fill faster?
