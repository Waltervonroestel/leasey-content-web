# Leasey.AI and SingleKey Partner to Deliver Automated Tenant Screening for Canadian Property Managers

*Integration closes the loop from first inquiry to signed lease, with compliance-aware screening built for Canadian regulation and rental platforms*

**VANCOUVER, BC, June 9, 2026** -- Leasey.AI, the AI-powered leasing automation platform for multifamily property managers, today announced an integration partnership with SingleKey, a leading Canadian tenant screening provider, bringing automated credit, identity, and income verification directly into the Leasey platform for Canadian portfolios.

The integration connects Leasey's end-to-end leasing funnel (listing syndication, AI-driven lead qualification, showing scheduling, and digital lease signing) with SingleKey's Canadian-specific screening layer, so property managers no longer need to step outside the platform to complete the qualification process.

**A market that rewards speed and accuracy**

Canada's rental market is moving fast in both directions. According to Yardi's Q1 2026 Multifamily Report, the national vacancy rate reached 5.1% with Calgary at 7.3% and Edmonton at 6.2%, giving operators more competition for qualified tenants and less margin for screening delays. At the same time, most leasing automation tools on the market are built for U.S. regulation and U.S. platforms, leaving Canadian operators to manage tenant verification through separate, province-specific workflows that sit outside their leasing stack.

The Leasey-SingleKey integration addresses that gap directly. SingleKey's reports cover Canadian credit bureau data, biometric identity verification, and employment and income validation, built for the regulatory requirements Canadian landlords actually face. Combined with Leasey's syndication to 48-plus marketplaces (including Kijiji, Apartments.com, and Facebook Marketplace), an operator can now move a prospect from first inquiry to a verified application without leaving the platform.

**Built for Canadian lease-up operators**

Canadian operators running high-volume portfolios, such as the 154-unit TEREZ luxury rental community in Cochrane, Alberta (managed by SunPeak Property Management, part of Ocgrow Group's Greystone development), represent the operators this integration is designed for: lease-up timelines where screening turnaround directly affects days on market and overall vacancy cost.
